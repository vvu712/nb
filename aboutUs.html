Beyond the Basics: Get To Know Our Company and Our Story

Background

Poor nutrition affects people of all walks of life. Poor nutrition further harms people's physical health and their mental health as well. A main contributor to poor nutrition is lack of access to healthy foods, yet those that have health options widely available to them rarely partake in healthy eating behaviors due to a lack of knowledge, motivation, and support.

Significance

Due to poor nutrition's affect on people's mental and physical health, interventions aimed at improving nutrition should treat both aspects. Although there are nutrition apps, many of them lack precision care. They are only fixated on counting calories, lack therapeutic approaches to target mental health challenges & behavior change, and have a narrow selection of cultural cuisines. This is why we have developed NutriWise Bot, an app that aims to promote healthier eating habits through a holistic approach.
