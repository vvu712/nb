THE TECHNOLOGY BEHIND NUTRIWISE BOT

Our Technology: Transparency and Trust in NutriWise Bot

How does NutriWise Bot work?

  • OpenLLaMA Integration: Leverages OpenLLama pretrained database.
  • Image Recognition: Employs image recognition technology and database to analyze food images and provide relevant nutritional information
  • Fine-Tuning for Precision:Refines its responses. Knowledge base backed up by nutrition professionals.
  • Integration with Nutritionix API: Database of nutrition information for common foods to develop accurate responses
